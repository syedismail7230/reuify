Automation

Robotic Program Automation (RPA)

With RPA, software users can create software robots, or "boots," that can learn, emulate, and run rules-based business processes. RPA automation allows users to create robots by tracking digital human actions. Let your robots see what they need to do, and then let them do the work. Robotic Process Automation software robots can communicate with any application or system in the same way as humans - except that RPA robots can work clockwise, continuously, quickly, and with 100% reliability and precision.

UiPath

UiPath creates software to automate the repetitive digital tasks that people typically perform. This technology combines to mimic how people read computer screens (AI Computer Vision) with APIs, giving users access to advanced automation components that can be combined to automate their own processes0,in internal and back-office areas such as accounting, personnel paperwork and complaint processing. New applications for the company's software include the coordination of artificial intelligence systems to simplify repetitive forward office tasks, including customer management.

TagUI

TagUI helps you quickly automate repetitive or time-critical tasks - use cases include process automation, data retrieval, and web application testing. The purpose of user interface (UI) automation is to make cognitive interactions available on the web and on your desktop. This is so that the computer can do it for you based on your schedule or status.
